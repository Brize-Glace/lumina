About

Join us for the first edition of Lumina FEST on May 23-24, 2025, at the Grande Halle de la Villette in Paris. This two-day event will bring together some of the biggest names in electronic music, featuring headliners like David Guetta, ILLENIUM, and Martin Garrix.

With 5 unique stages and an unforgettable lineup blending house, melodic bass, dubstep, and more, Lumina FEST is set to deliver an electrifying experience for music lovers.

Get ready to feel the energy, live the music, and celebrate EDM like never before.

For those aged 14 to 15, the legal guardian must also present their ID. Attendees aged 16 to 17 must bring their valid ID, along with a photocopy of their parent’s or legal guardian’s ID (front and back) and a signed parental authorization form, available for download on our website. For attendees aged 18 and over, a valid ID alone is sufficient. Please note that entry will be denied without the required documentation, and no refunds will be issued in such cases.

We highly encourage attendees to use public transportation to reduce their carbon footprint. The Grande Halle de la Villette is easily accessible by metro, tram, and bus. The closest metro stations are Porte de Pantin (Line 5) and Corentin Cariou (Line 7), both within walking distance. Tram lines T3b and bus lines 75, 151, 139, and 150 also stop nearby. If you prefer biking, there are bike parking areas around the venue. For those coming by car, parking is available, but public transport is recommended for convenience and environmental reasons. You can find detailed information on routes and maps on the official website of La Villette.

There are several great hotels near the Grande Halle de la Villette, offering a range of options for different budgets. You can consider staying at the Hotel Mercure Paris 19 Philharmonie La Villette, just a short walk from the venue, which offers modern amenities for a comfortable stay. Another option is the Hotel Restaurant Au Boeuf Couronné, a traditional hotel with a restaurant, located near Parc de la Villette. For those who prefer more independent accommodation, Appart'City Classic La Villette provides fully-equipped apartments, ideal for longer stays or those who want more flexibility. If you're looking for an affordable and well-located option, Hotel Ibis Paris La Villette Cité des Sciences 19ème offers simple yet comfortable rooms with easy access to public transport. We recommend booking your accommodation in advance, especially during busy periods, to ensure availability.
